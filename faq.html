FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Rotum Protocol architecture, rules, and reward frameworks.

What is Rotum Protocol?+
Rotum Protocol is a community-driven contribution ecosystem built on The Open Network (TON). Moving beyond simple tap-to-earn models, Rotum utilizes a contribution-based framework where users build long-term digital identity through active ecosystem participation.
What is the total supply of RTM?+
The total maximum supply is fixed at 1,000,000 RTM tokens. Additional token minting is permanently disabled.
What are the utilities of the RTM token?+
RTM acts as the core utility asset within the ecosystem. It is used for contribution boosts, multiplier enhancements, locking rewards, participating in seasonal ecosystem events, achievement progression, and community governance.
How does the contribution and achievement system work?+
Users interact with the platform primarily via the Telegram Mini App. As development progresses into later phases, user participation and seasonal achievements will be verified via on-chain smart contracts, utilizing achievement NFT systems and Soulbound contributor badges to create permanent digital identity metrics.
How are rewards distributed?+
60% of the total RTM allocation is dedicated directly to the community. Rewards are distributed through seasonal campaigns, contributor pools, and participation incentives based on objective engagement tracking.
Are rewards guaranteed?+
No. Rotum Protocol is under active development. Ecosystem participation does not guarantee financial returns, profits, or future market value. Nothing within our literature represents legal, financial, or investment advice.
