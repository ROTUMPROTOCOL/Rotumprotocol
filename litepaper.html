Introduction

Rotum Protocol is a community-driven contribution ecosystem built on The Open Network (TON). Unlike traditional tap-to-earn models, Rotum introduces a contribution-based participation framework where users actively engage with the ecosystem through participation, collaboration, and long-term contribution activities.

The protocol is designed to reward meaningful ecosystem participation while promoting sustainability, transparency, and digital ownership. Through RTM, the native utility token of the ecosystem, participants can access various features including boosts, contribution enhancements, achievement systems, and future ecosystem services.

Rotum Protocol aims to create a sustainable participation economy where contributors are recognized, rewarded, and empowered within a transparent digital ecosystem.

Vision

Our vision is to build a decentralized participation ecosystem where user contributions create measurable value and long-term digital identity.

Rotum Protocol seeks to evolve beyond traditional reward systems by combining contribution mechanics, achievement systems, digital ownership, and blockchain infrastructure to create an ecosystem centered around participation, reputation, and community growth.
