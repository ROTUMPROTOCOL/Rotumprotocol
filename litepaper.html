Introduction

Rotum Protocol is a decentralized contribution, reputation, and trust infrastructure built on The Open Network (TON). The protocol enables projects, communities, and participants to establish verifiable digital reputation through contribution tracking, participation history, and trust scoring.

Unlike traditional reward platforms that prioritize short-term incentives, Rotum introduces a sustainable ecosystem where users build measurable on-chain and off-chain reputation through meaningful participation.

Rotum combines contribution tracking, reputation scoring, contributor identity, anti-Sybil mechanisms, and ecosystem rewards into a unified protocol designed to support the next generation of decentralized communities on TON.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading contribution and trust infrastructure layer within the TON ecosystem.

Rotum seeks to create a decentralized environment where contributions are measurable, reputation is portable, rewards are transparent, and communities can identify and reward genuine participants.

Problem Statement

The blockchain ecosystem faces significant structural challenges including widespread Sybil attacks, unfair or easily-gamed airdrops, an absence of reliable contributor reputation systems, fragmented reward mechanisms, and difficulty identifying genuine long-term supporters. Rotum addresses these challenges directly through its modular contribution-based reputation infrastructure.

Rotum Trust & Reputation Protocol

The core protocol engine comprehensively evaluates ecosystem users utilizing three foundational operational pillars:

  • Wallet Reputation: Historical analysis of on-chain activity, transaction patterns, and liquidity footprint within the TON ecosystem.
  • Contribution Power (CP): A dynamic metric representing user participation velocity, continuous engagement, and active contribution efforts.
  • Community Reputation: Measures the depth of social coordination, project engagement, and localized ecosystem support.

Core Protocol Features

Rotum Trust Score

Calculated dynamically via a proprietary formula:

Trust Score = Wallet Reputation + Contribution Power + Community Participation + Referral Quality + Achievement History

This highly verifiable aggregate score can be used externally by TON projects, airdrop campaigns, specific community programs, and tokenized reward systems to filter out bad actors.

Contributor Passport

Each participant receives a portable contributor identity profile containing:

  • Verifiable Trust Score
  • Contribution Power rankings
  • On-chain achievements & badges
  • Seasonal global rankings & reputation level
  • Referral performance metrics & history

Anti-Sybil Infrastructure

To identify and guarantee genuine user interaction, Rotum implements multi-layered protective frameworks:

  • Deep wallet footprint analysis
  • Transaction pattern anomaly detection
  • Historical user participation validity
  • Continuous activity consistency checks
  • Proof-of-contribution verifications
