Contribution Framework

Hash Contribution

Ecosystem contribution framework metrics, processing scores and operational tier rules.

Framework

Rotum Protocol uses raw processing indicators to track community participation across separate contribution windows. Your cumulative Hash Contribution determines network weights and allocations.

Seasonal Rewards

Rewards are distributed according to:

  • Total Hash Contribution
  • Contribution Share
  • Operator Rank
  • Loyalty Score
  • Seasonal Events
  • Ecosystem Participation
Participation in the ecosystem does not guarantee rewards, returns, or compensation. Any rewards distributed are determined by contribution activity, ecosystem conditions, and protocol governance.
