Distribution Logic

Seasonal rewards are calculated dynamically at the end of each operational block phase. The total pool size changes over time depending on active contributions and node uptime parameters.

Transparency

All seasonal distributions will be publicly verifiable. Reward calculations are based on predefined rules and contribution metrics.

Participation in Rotum Protocol does not guarantee financial returns. Rewards depend on ecosystem activity, participation and seasonal distribution rules.
